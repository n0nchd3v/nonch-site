What's inside

Everything a gym-goer
actually needs.

Built around the things that keep you consistent — not a bloated wellness app.

01
Workout Tracking
Log every set, rep, and weight in real time. Build custom workouts, schedule sessions, and see your full training history at a glance.
02
Calorie & Macro Tracking
Log meals across Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, and Snacks. Set daily calorie and macro goals and track progress against them every day.
03
Streaks
Keep your workout streak, calorie streak, and post streak alive. Lose it once and start over — the pressure is the point.
04
Points & Leaderboards
Earn points for every session logged, streak maintained, and goal hit. Compete on the leaderboard and see how you rank against the community.
05
Social Feed
Share workouts, nutrition wins, and milestones with your followers. Like, comment, and keep each other accountable on a feed built for gym-goers.

Exercise Library
Browse a curated library of exercises with demo videos. Filter by muscle group, equipment, or difficulty and add straight to your workout.

Workout Tracking

Log every set.
Beat every record.

Build workouts from scratch or from templates. Log sets, reps, and weights in real time at the gym. gymmit. automatically tracks personal records so every session gives you something to chase.

  • Custom workout builder
  • Live session logging with rest timers
  • Automatic personal record tracking
  • Full history and volume trends
  • Workout scheduling

Calorie & Nutrition

Hit your macros.
Every single day.

Log food meal by meal — Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, and Snacks. Search millions of foods, save your go-tos, and build meal templates for things you eat regularly. Your calorie and macro goals are always front and centre.

  • Meal-by-meal food logging
  • Daily calorie and macro goals
  • Saved foods and meal templates
  • Powered by the FatSecret food database
  • Full nutrition history by date

Points & Leaderboards

Earn points.
Climb the board.

Every workout logged, every streak maintained, and every goal hit earns you points. The leaderboard shows exactly where you stand against everyone else — the motivation to keep going is built in.

  • Points for workouts, streaks, and goals
  • Community leaderboard rankings
  • Workout streaks, calorie streaks, post streaks
  • Streak recovery and milestone tracking

Social

Train with people
who get it.

Share sessions, photos, and progress to a feed of people who actually go to the gym. Follow friends, react to posts, and let your streak do the talking. Public or private — you control what you share.

  • Public feed and post sharing
  • Workout and streak posts
  • Likes, comments, and follows
  • Private and public post controls
  • Profile with avatar, cover, and stats
