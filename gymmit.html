Workout Tracking

Log every set.
Beat every record.

Build workouts from scratch or from templates. Log sets, reps, and weights in real time at the gym. gymmit. automatically tracks personal records so every session gives you something to chase.

  • Custom workout builder
  • Live session logging with rest timers
  • Automatic personal record tracking
  • Full history and volume trends
  • Workout scheduling

Calorie & Nutrition

Hit your macros.
Every single day.

Log food meal by meal — Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, and Snacks. Search millions of foods, save your go-tos, and build meal templates for things you eat regularly. Your calorie and macro goals are always front and centre.

  • Meal-by-meal food logging
  • Daily calorie and macro goals
  • Saved foods and meal templates
  • Powered by the FatSecret food database
  • Full nutrition history by date

Points & Leaderboards

Earn points.
Climb the board.

Every workout logged, every streak maintained, and every goal hit earns you points. The leaderboard shows exactly where you stand against everyone else — the motivation to keep going is built in.

  • Points for workouts, streaks, and goals
  • Community leaderboard rankings
  • Workout streaks, calorie streaks, post streaks
  • Streak recovery and milestone tracking

Pro · AI Training Plan

Your goals.
Your plan.

Tell gymmit. your goal — hypertrophy, fat loss, strength, endurance, sport-specific, or general fitness. It builds a full personalised workout and nutrition plan around your experience, schedule, exercise preferences, and dietary needs. Powered by AI, tailored to you.

  • Natural language goal input
  • Full workout split with sets, reps, rest, and RPE
  • Daily meal plans with macros and ingredients
  • Training & rest day calorie cycling
  • Exercise swaps and plan progression
